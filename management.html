Experience IS the difference.

Company owner and operator, Mara Schoenberg, founded Dental Fill-Ins® in 1999. Since then she has been providing top-quality dental personnel to dental practices throughout Nassau, Suffolk and The Five Boroughs. Mara’s knowledge and hands-on approach is evident in her attention to your specific needs.

With more than 25 years of dental-industry experience behind us, we distinguish ourselves by understanding the value of building long-term relationships with you, our clients and personnel. Our Dental Fill-Ins® professionals consists of top-quality people who are specifically trained to best serve your dental office needs. Mara’s experience, vision, and commitment to excellence are obvious to all of her clients.

Mara Schoenberg, President

Company Owner and operator, Mara Schoenberg, founded Dental Fill-Ins® after working for 15 years as a dental Office Manager. While employed as the Office Manager, Mara struggled with finding quality, reliable staff through the various agencies.

Mara saw a need and Dental Fill-Ins® was born. Dental professionals now have a place to call and get the quality of staff and service they need. A place they can rely on; where an informed and capable person will answer the phone and be able to help staff their dental office. She succeeded. And now, almost two decades later, Dental Fill-Ins® is the first choice for thousands of dental professionals.
